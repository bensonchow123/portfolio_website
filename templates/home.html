Hi there! I'm Benson Chow. This site is where I share my projects and hobbies.

I love 3D printing and learning CAD, mostly using my QIDI Q1 Pro coreXY printer and Kingroon filament dryer to make custom parts for my other interests.

I'm also into high-fidelity audio and collect FLAC music. To manage my library, I built a personal streaming service on a Raspberry Pi, and I even track every track I play—you can see the stats on the Music page.

Another hobby is building and modding mechanical keyboards—my favorite build so far is a customized Keychron Q3.

Programming ties all these interests together. I daily-drive an Arch Linux system (LUKS-encrypted BTRFS with KDE Plasma), which keeps me learning about coding, system administration, and open-source tools.

This site is built with Python Flask, hosted on a free server, and running on a free .cf domain (yep, straight from the Central African Republic!).
You can read more about projects mentioned in the Projects page!
